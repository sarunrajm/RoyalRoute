SPECIAL EUROPEAN TOUR

Glastonbury Festival (United Kingdom)

Held in Somerset, England, Glastonbury is one of the largest and most iconic music festivals in the world. It features a wide range of musical genres, including rock, pop, electronic, and more.<

Tomorrowland (Belgium)

Tomorrowland is a massive electronic dance music festival held in Boom, Belgium. Known for its elaborate stage designs and impressive lineup of international DJs, it attracts music enthusiasts from around the globe.

Oktoberfest (Germany)

Held annually in Munich, Oktoberfest is the world's largest beer festival. Visitors can enjoy traditional Bavarian food, music, and, of course, beer in large beer tents and experience the festive atmosphere.
